Skill Development Programs (SDP)

SDPs at VIT focus on enhancing students' practical skills through workshops and hands-on training. These programs bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and industry requirements.

Entrepreneurship Development Initiatives (EDI)

EDIs cultivate entrepreneurial skills by providing mentorship, funding opportunities, and guidance to students aiming to create startups and innovative solutions.

Design Thinking (DT)

DT emphasizes a solution-oriented approach to problems. Students use creative strategies and collaborative teamwork to address real-world challenges.

Course Projects

Course projects at VIT are an integral part of the curriculum, helping students apply their knowledge to create tangible outcomes, often in collaboration with industry partners.
